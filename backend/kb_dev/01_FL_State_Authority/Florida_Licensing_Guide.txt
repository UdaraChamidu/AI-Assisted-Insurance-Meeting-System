
FLORIDA DEPARTMENT OF FINANCIAL SERVICES
Bureau of Licensing

TOPIC: BECOMING AN INSURANCE AGENT
AUTHORITY LEVEL: STATE LAW

1. Residency
You must be a resident of Florida or a resident of a state with a reciprocal agreement.

2. Pre-Licensing Education
You must complete a 60-hour pre-licensing course for Life & Health (2-15 license).

3. Examination
You must pass the state examination with a score of 70% or higher.

4. Fingerprinting
All applicants must undergo a background check.
